Origin-destination flow map generated by Flox

Flox

Flox is a tool for creating origin-destination flow maps. It automatically changes the shape of flows in order to reduce flow intersections and avoid excessive flow overlap. This results in maps that are easier to read than flow maps that use straight lines.

Flox was developed at Oregon State University by Bernard Jenny, Daniel Stephen, Ritesh Sharma, and Eugene Zhang
