Flox

Flox is a mapping application for creating origin-destination flow maps. It includes a method for automatically laying out curved flow lines, which arranges the flows in order to reduce flow intersection and avoid excessive flow overlap. This results in maps that are easier to read than flow maps using straight lines. Additional tools for manually customizing the map's layout and appearance are also included in the application.

Flox was developed at Oregon State University by Bernard Jenny, Daniel Stephen, Ritesh Sharma, and Eugene Zhang
